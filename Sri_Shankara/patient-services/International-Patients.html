Global Cancer Care

At Sri Shankara Cancer Hospital & Research Centre, we are more than just a hospital; we are a beacon of hope for patients worldwide. As a national-level referral centre, we combine cutting-edge technology, world-renowned oncologists, and a profound commitment to compassion to deliver seamless, end-to-end care for our international patients.

Your Personalized Care Journey Starts Here

Effortless Medical History Analysis

  • Simply share your medical records online.
  • Our world-class experts will meticulously review your case and recommend the most effective, personalized treatment options.
  • Receive a comprehensive, tailored care roadmap before you even begin your journey.
  • Global Standards, Local Compassion: Our tumour boards adhere to stringent international protocols, guaranteeing you the same exceptional level of care found in leading global centres.

Confidential Pre-Travel E-Consultation

  • Connect directly with your specialist through a secure video consultation from the comfort of your home.
  • Gain a clear understanding of your diagnosis, proposed treatment plan, and transparent cost estimates.
  • Ask every question, build complete confidence, and make informed travel decisions with peace of mind.

Comprehensive Travel & Visa Concierge

  • Our dedicated International Desk provides full support for visa applications and all necessary paperwork.
  • Enjoy complimentary airport pickup/drop-off and personalized assistance with hotel bookings for you and your family.
  • Benefit from transparent financial counselling and clear cost estimates, ensuring no hidden surprises.
  • Unmatched Value: We proudly offer an unparalleled combination of affordability and global excellence in cancer care.

Empathetic Multilingual Translation Services

  • Access in-house translators fluent in Arabic, French, Swahili, Russian, and many more languages, ensuring clear communication at every step.
  • Receive dedicated cultural liaison support to cater to your dietary, religious, and personal comfort needs, making you feel at home.

Streamlined Admission & Discharge Experience

  • A dedicated Patient Care Executive will guide you through a smooth admission, billing, and discharge process.
  • Receive comprehensive family assistance services throughout your inpatient stay, ensuring their comfort and support.
  • Benefit from streamlined medical documentation, facilitating hassle-free post-travel insurance and reimbursement.

Continuous Follow-Up E-Consultation

  • Maintain a strong, ongoing connection with your treating doctor online, no matter where you are.
  • Receive expert post-treatment monitoring, personalized medication advice, and essential recovery guidance.
  • Experience accessible, anytime care – long after you return home, ensuring your continued well-being.
