Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 07-09-2025

Sri Shankara Cancer Hospital & Research Centre (SSCHRC) is committed to protecting the privacy and confidentiality of visitors to our website. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information. By using our website, you consent to the practices described here.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

a) Personal Information (when voluntarily submitted)

  • Name, phone number, email address, postal address
  • Date of birth, gender (if provided in forms)
  • Medical or appointment-related details you submit through online forms

b) Non-Personal Information (collected automatically)

  • IP address, browser type, device information
  • Website usage data (pages visited, time spent, referring website)
  • Cookies or similar tracking technologies

2. How We Use Your Information

We may use the collected information for purposes such as:

  • Responding to inquiries and appointment requests
  • Providing information about hospital services and facilities
  • Improving website functionality and user experience
  • Internal research, reporting, and analytics
  • Compliance with legal or regulatory requirements

We will never sell or rent your personal information to third parties.

3. Data Security

  • We use reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect personal information.
  • However, no online transmission or storage system is 100% secure; we cannot guarantee absolute security.

4. Sharing of Information

We may share information only in these limited circumstances:

  • With authorised hospital staff for scheduling and patient support
  • With trusted service providers who assist in website hosting or IT operations (bound by confidentiality agreements)
  • When required by law, regulation, or court order

5. Cookies & Tracking

  • Our website may use cookies and similar technologies to improve user experience.
  • You may disable cookies in your browser, but some website features may not function properly.

6. Your Rights

Depending on applicable law, you may have the right to:

  • Access, update, or correct your personal information
  • Request deletion of your data (subject to legal obligations)
  • Withdraw consent for certain uses of your information
  • Opt out of non-essential communications

Requests can be made by contacting us (see details below).

7. Children's Privacy

  • Our website is not directed at children under 18.
  • We do not knowingly collect personal information from children without parental/guardian consent.

8. International Visitors

While this website is primarily intended for users in India, visitors from other countries acknowledge that their information will be processed in India, where data protection laws may differ.

9. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last Updated" date at the top will reflect changes. Please review this page periodically.
