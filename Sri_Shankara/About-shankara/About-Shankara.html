Sri Shankara Cancer Hospital and Research Centre

Namaskara,

At Sri Shankara Cancer Hospital and Research Centre, we began with a simple yet powerful belief: that quality cancer care should not be a privilege, but a right for every Indian, regardless of their background or means.

When this hospital was founded, it was not just built with brick and mortar, it was built on compassion, ethics, and a deep sense of service. Inspired by the blessings of Jagadguru Sri Sri Sri Bharati Tirtha Mahaswamiji and Godess Mother Sharada of Sringeri, our vision was to create a cancer hospital that offers world-class care, steeped in Indian values, where every patient feels safe, heard, and supported.

Over the years, we've grown into a leading comprehensive cancer centre in India, offering cutting-edge treatments, advanced diagnostics, holistic patient support and also cancer prevention and cancer research, all under one roof. Even as we embrace the latest in science and technology, we remain deeply rooted in our mission:

To serve with compassion. To treat with dignity. To heal with hope.

As a surgeon, educator, and fellow human being, I've seen firsthand how a timely diagnosis, the right treatment, and a kind word can transform outcomes. That's why we place equal emphasis on awareness, early detection, education, and research—alongside clinical care.

To every patient and caregiver who walks through our doors:
You are not alone. We are with you—every step of the way.

With gratitude and commitment,
Dr. B.S. Srinath
Senior Consultant, Surgical Oncologist & Head of the Institution

Sri Shankara Cancer Hospital and Research Centre is a leading tertiary care cancer facility, located in the heart of Bengaluru, Karnataka.

We offer integrated, multidisciplinary cancer care—from early screening and diagnosis to advanced treatments like surgery, chemotherapy, immunotherapy, radiation, and bone marrow transplantation. Our facilities include:

  • Advanced Robotic Surgery: Sri Shankara Cancer Hospital is equipped with state-of-the-art robotic surgical systems, enabling highly precise, minimally invasive cancer surgeries
  • Modern radiotherapy units, including linear accelerators and brachytherapy
  • Modular operation theatres equipped for complex oncological surgeries
  • Dedicated Day Care Chemotherapy and Inpatient Units
  • A fully equipped Oncopathology and Molecular Diagnostics Lab
  • NABH-accredited standards of quality and safety
  • Research and academic infrastructure for clinical trials and cancer education

We serve all sections of society, ensuring that financial constraints do not deny access to quality cancer treatment, thanks to our charitable arm and the generosity of donors.

Who We Are

At Sri Shankara Cancer Hospital and Research Centre, we are dedicated to one mission: Delivering world-class, compassionate cancer care at an environment that feels like a second home..

We are deeply committed to raising awareness about cancer, educating communities, and reaching even the most remote corners of India, with the vision of a cancer-free nation in the near future.

Founded with the blessings of Jagadguru Sri Sri Sri Bharati Tirtha Mahaswamiji and Godess Mother Sharada of Sringeri and rooted in the timeless values of seva (service) and karuna (compassion), our hospital stands as a beacon of hope, healing, and humanity for patients from across the country and beyond.

As a not-for-profit, comprehensive cancer centre located in Bengaluru, we are devoted to the holistic advancement of cancer treatment, research, and education—all under one roof.

Our approach blends cutting-edge medical innovation with the wisdom of empathy, ensuring that every patient is cared for not just as a case, but as a human being with a story, a family, and a future.

Our Leadership

At the helm of Sri Shankara Cancer Hospital and Research Centre is a leadership team grounded in clinical excellence, ethical commitment, and a deep sense of service. Our governing board, medical directors, and administrative heads bring decades of experience from India's most respected healthcare institutions.

The hospital was envisioned and nurtured under the spiritual guidance of Jagadguru Sri Sri Sri Bharati Tirtha Mahaswamiji, the 36th Pontiff of the Sringeri Sharada Peetham and Jagadguru Sri Sri Vidhushekhara Bharati Mahaswamiji.

Our clinical leadership includes renowned oncologists, surgeons, radiation experts, pathologists, and scientists, many of whom are leaders in their fields nationally and internationally. This multidisciplinary expertise ensures evidence-based care tailored to every patient's unique needs.
