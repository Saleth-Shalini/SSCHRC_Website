Department of Onco-Nephrology

At Sri Shankara Cancer Foundation, our Onco-Nephrology Department specializes in protecting kidney function in patients undergoing cancer therapy. We work closely with oncologists to prevent, detect, and manage kidney complications caused by cancer or treatments such as chemotherapy, targeted therapy, and immunotherapy.

Healthy kidneys are essential for safely metabolizing and eliminating most cancer drugs. Our goal is to preserve renal function, avoid unnecessary dose reductions, and ensure patients can complete the most effective treatment plan.

Key Services

Comprehensive nephrology care for cancer patients

Acute Kidney Injury (AKI) & CKD

Rapid diagnosis and treatment of AKI or worsening CKD from tumor lysis, dehydration, or drug toxicity.

Maintains treatment continuity and protects long-term kidney health.

Immunotherapy Nephrotoxicity

Management of immune checkpoint inhibitor–related acute interstitial nephritis.

Enables safe resumption of immunotherapy when appropriate.

Targeted Therapy & Chemotherapy Toxicity

Mitigation of nephrotoxic effects from cisplatin, VEGF inhibitors, and other agents.

Reduces risk of irreversible damage and improves tolerability.

Electrolyte & Hypertension Control

Correction of imbalances and management of therapy-induced hypertension.

Restores balance and prevents complications.

Renal Replacement Therapy

Hemodialysis or peritoneal dialysis for severe AKI or end-stage disease.

Supports ongoing cancer treatment or transplant eligibility.

Precision Dosing & Nephroprotection

Accurate GFR estimation and personalized hydration protocols.

Ensures safe, effective drug dosing.

Distinctive Strengths

Integrated Multidisciplinary Care

Active participation in tumor boards for real-time input on treatment selection and dosing.

Expertise in Emerging Toxicities

Specialized management of complications from immunotherapy, CAR T-cell therapy, and targeted agents.

Survivorship Monitoring

Long-term follow-up for delayed kidney issues after high-risk therapies.

Patient-Centered Approach

Individualized plans with clear communication and shared decision-making.
