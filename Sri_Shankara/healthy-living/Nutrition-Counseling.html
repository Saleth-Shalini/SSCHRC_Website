Nutrition Counseling

Our clinical dietitians provide evidence-based nutrition counseling for patients undergoing cancer treatment, survivors, and caregivers. Sessions are designed around your diagnosis, treatment plan, symptoms, and personal food preferences.

Personalized Assessment

Review of medical history, weight trends, appetite, digestion, lab values, and current eating patterns to create a practical care plan.

Meal Planning Support

Guidance on calorie and protein needs, tube feeding support when required, and culturally suitable home-based meal ideas.

Symptom Management

Strategies for nausea, mouth sores, constipation, diarrhoea, taste changes, and low energy during chemotherapy or radiation.

Who Can Benefit?

Patients in Active Treatment

  • Those starting chemotherapy, immunotherapy, or radiation
  • Patients with unplanned weight loss or poor intake
  • Individuals preparing for or recovering from surgery

Survivors & Wellness

  • Long-term survivors seeking healthy weight management
  • Caregivers needing guidance on nourishing meals at home
  • Anyone referred by their oncologist for dietetic review
