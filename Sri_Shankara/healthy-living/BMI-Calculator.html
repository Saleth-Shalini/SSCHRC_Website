BMI Calculator

Body Mass Index (BMI) is a screening tool that estimates whether your weight is in a healthy range for your height. It does not diagnose health conditions on its own, but it can help start a conversation with your care team.

Understanding Your Result

Under 18.5

Underweight. Discuss balanced nutrition and screening with a dietitian, especially during or after cancer treatment.

18.5 – 24.9

Normal weight range for most adults. Continue healthy eating and regular activity as advised by your doctor.

25 – 29.9

Overweight. Lifestyle guidance and medical review may help reduce long-term health risks.

30 and above

Obesity range. A personalized nutrition and activity plan with your healthcare team is recommended.
