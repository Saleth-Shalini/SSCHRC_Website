Professional Overview

Dr. Venkata Prabhakara Rao R. is a seasoned healthcare leader with over four decades of experience in clinical medicine, hospital administration, and healthcare quality improvement. He holds an MBBS, DA, PGD in Hospital & Health Management, PGD in Medico Legal Systems, and is a NABH Certified Assessor. He brings deep expertise in managing large multidisciplinary healthcare institutions and a strong commitment to excellence in patient care.

Throughout his career, Dr. Prabhakara Rao has held senior leadership roles in prominent hospitals, where he has driven operational efficiency, strengthened clinical governance, and enhanced patient-centric services. His background combines clinical insight with strategic management skills, making him a respected figure in hospital leadership and healthcare delivery.

In his current role as Director of Sri Shankara Cancer Hospital & Research Centre, Dr. Prabhakara Rao is focused on advancing the institution's mission to provide compassionate, high-quality, and affordable cancer care. He leads initiatives to improve systems, foster a culture of continuous improvement, and ensure the highest standards of clinical excellence across all areas of the hospital.

Under his guidance, the hospital continues to grow its impact and deepen its commitment to serving patients and families with empathy, integrity, and professionalism.
