Professional Overview

Dr. Chandrakant B. Patil is a highly experienced clinician in the Department of Cardiology, with over 40 years of dedicated service. He holds an MD in Medicine and a DM in Cardiology, specializing primarily in non-invasive cardiology with a strong focus on prevention and early detection of heart diseases.

He is a respected member of the Association of Physicians of India and holds fellowships in the Cardiological Society of India, Indian College of Cardiology, and Indian Academy of Echocardiography. His academic contributions include publishing over 67 articles in state, national, and international journals, as well as authoring chapters in landmark cardiology reference works, including Acute Rheumatic Fever and Rheumatic Heart Disease and Congenital Heart Disease, edited by Dr. I. B. Vijayalakshmi
