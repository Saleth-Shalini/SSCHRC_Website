Professional Overview

Dr. Surekha T M is a Junior Consultant in Paediatric Oncology at Sri Shankara Cancer Hospital & Research Centre, Bengaluru. She completed her MBBS, MD in Paediatrics (May 2021), and DM in Paediatric Oncology (November 2025). She is dedicated to providing compassionate and evidence-based care to children with cancer and blood disorders.

Dr. Surekha is committed to holistic care for paediatric oncology patients and their families. Her focus includes diagnosis, treatment planning, and supportive care for children undergoing cancer treatment, in line with the centre's multidisciplinary approach to paediatric oncology and BMT.
